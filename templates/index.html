Bubble Chart

Bubble Chart for each country along with their respective data: Country Name, Happiness Score, Covid Cases, and Deaths as of the end of 2020.

02

Demographic Information

Demographic Information for individual country. The user can select the country and select the Country, ID, Type of Government, 2020 Population, and the World Region.

03

World Map

World Map for all countries within the respected dataset. The user can select to observe Happiness Scores or The Number of Deaths for Each Country. By clicking on any circle, the user will observe a popup to see the Country Name and Happiness Score or Number of Deaths.

  • Happiness Report 2020

    The Happiness Score is a rank given to each country based entirely on the survey scores from 2020, using the Gallup weights to make the estimates representative. There are six factors surveyed - levels of GDP, life expectancy, generosity, social support, freedom, and corruption - are estimated to contribute to making life evaluations higher in each country.   • Covid-19

    According to the World Health Organization "Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness."


    You can find the world covid dataset from Our World In Data, (OWID) "The complete COVID-19 dataset is a collection of the COVID-19 data maintained by Our World in Data. It is updated daily and includes data on confirmed cases, deaths, and testing."

  • Government Responses

    COVID-19 government responses, CoronaNet Research Project "A research collaboration of more than 400 researchers around the world tracking policy events in response to COVID-19"

    The dataset can be found in data.world.
